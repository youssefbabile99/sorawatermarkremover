Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data Collection

1.1 Share Link Processing

When you submit a video share link, we process it in real time to fetch the watermark-free video. We do not store the video content itself. Only the submitted URL is temporarily logged for the duration of the request.

1.2 Network & Abuse Prevention

We collect minimal server logs (IP addresses, request timestamps, user-agent strings) to prevent abuse and ensure service reliability. These logs are automatically deleted after 30 days.

1.3 Usage Metrics

We may collect anonymized usage statistics (such as total number of requests) to improve our service. This data cannot be used to identify individual users.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used exclusively for:

  • Processing your watermark removal requests
  • Preventing abuse and ensuring fair usage
  • Improving service reliability and performance
  • Technical troubleshooting

3. Data Storage & Security

We do not store video files. Processing happens in real-time and video data is streamed directly from the source to your browser. Server logs are stored securely and automatically purged after 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

Our tool may interact with third-party APIs to process watermark removal requests. We do not share personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Cookies

This website may use essential cookies for basic functionality. We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies. See our Cookie Policy for details.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request information about data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of any non-essential data collection

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

Questions about your privacy? Contact us at our contact page.
