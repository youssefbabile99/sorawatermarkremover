Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your experience.

Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

We may use cookies strictly necessary for the website to function properly. These cannot be disabled without breaking core functionality.

Analytics Cookies

We may use anonymized analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website (e.g., pages visited, session duration). This data is aggregated and cannot identify you personally.

Cookies We Do NOT Use

  • Advertising cookies: We do not serve ads or use ad-tracking cookies
  • Social media tracking cookies: We do not embed social media trackers
  • Third-party marketing cookies: We do not share data with marketing platforms

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Note that blocking essential cookies may affect site functionality.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this cookie policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page.
